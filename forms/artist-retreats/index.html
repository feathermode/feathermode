FONTS

Type Pairs

Think of your website in two font formats one font for your headings and larger text and one font for your paragraphs and small text. They can be the same font or two different fonts. Fonts are usually categorized by style. Serif fonts have more edges and intricate points in their lettering while Sans Serif fonts tend to be rounder, smoother and less complex. Your selections will help me curate a handful of font pairings for you to choose from.

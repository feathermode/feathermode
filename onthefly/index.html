ontheFLY

Your Web Preferences, Our Craftsmanship

ontheFLY is feathermode's smart templating and semi-custom web creation process. ontheFLY is best used for small to medium sites and is geared towards content creators and small businesses. If you need to quickly create a website with a small selection of needs, but don't want to spend the time it takes to reformat and tinker with templates from web builders, ontheFLY is the right service for you.

If you'd like a more custom website with greater functionality and scalability consider using feathermode's Gold service which involves bespoke and truly custom experience through every step of the process and a 1-1 partnership between you and a web architect.
